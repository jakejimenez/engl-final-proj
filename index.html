What people are saying about DST

Summary

Storytelling has been utilized as an effective means of communication for years. Digital Storytelling can also be a potent tool for students who are taught to create their own stories. An engaging, multimedia-rich digital story can serve as an anticipatory set or hook to capture the attention of students and increase their interest in exploring new ideas. Students are able to share in a way that quickly instills a positive classroom environment and draws attention to student voices. Put simply, "Because various educational applications of digital storytelling are in compliance with how human beings learn, researchers have found that digital storytelling has played an effective role in many aspects of education." (Wang 79)

What you need to know about DST

Benefits of Digital Storytelling

Improved Visual Memory Skills

Many are familar with memory and the process by which we retain and recall certain events in our lives and experiences. VM or Visual Memory "is the ability to recall or remember images, scenes, words or other information presented visually" (Sarica 299). Throughout the research of this topic, multiple sources outlined significant improvement in Visual Memory Capacity (VMC) during and after the utilization of DST. A study done tested the improvement of VMC and showed "that there was significant development at the .05 level for both the experimental and the control groups regarding the students' VMC (3.81 > 1.70, that is tH > t29, 0.05 and 2.84 > 1.70, thus tH > t28, 0.05)" (Sarica 305).

Improved Writing Skills

Writing is one of the most common and cognitvely stimulating activities one can participate in, and skills for such an activity are utilized throughout one's life. Digital Storytelling teaches students the power of combining various media styles within their work, and shows the power of technology when utilized creatively. With DST, teachers can keep students "fully engaged" throughout the writing process. Multiple sources confirmed the improvement of writing skills when used in conjunction with DST, one stated "a significant development at the .05 significance level in both the experimental and the control groups regarding the students' WSs (3.68>1.70, that is tH>t29, 0.05 and 6.39>1.70 thus tH>t28, 0.05)... this development is mainly to the advantage of the experimental group" (Sarica 307). Where "WSs" stands for Writing Skills'.

Promotes In-Class Discussion

DST has many effective benefits, one of the many is the promotion it lends to in-class discussions. The usage of DST empowers students to "create a story to share their experiences, report their findings, reflect on their understandings" (Wang 78), all the while enriching discussion by creating an environment students feel comfortable sharing in.

Enhances Critical Thinking

Critical Thinking is imperative in a genre such as Digital Storytelling, an especially important aspect when applied to the storytelling project process. Understanding audience, content, and complex topics all require the utilization of critical thinking during the process. Fortunately for educators, involving DST into the classroom environment has had an enhanced impact on students critical thinking skills. In a survey conducted by Pelin Yüksel Arslan at the University of Vienna, nearly 71% of survey participants reported improved critical thinking skills as a result of a DST project (Arslan 6).

Promotes Academic Achievement

Academic Achivement is perhaps one of the most important benefits to utilizing DST in classroom environments. These achivemenents have been documented to have occured within many different subject areas, and have varying levels of impact on students. One of the most successful DST subjects has been English, where students had significant improvement when learning the language when paired with storytelling projects. One study concluded "the partial eta squared of .28, we can conclude that DST had a significantly large main effect on academic achievement in English" (Yang 346).

Exposure to Digital Toolsets

One of the most apparent advantages of DST is the exposure students recieve to various digital tools. Tools including video editing software, programming editors, and data visualization/presentation applications available on the internet. The increased exposure to digital technology allows for students to become more familar with tools that will undoubtedly be utilized throughout their careers. The effect not only benefits their education but their ability to be successful in other aspects concering technology.
